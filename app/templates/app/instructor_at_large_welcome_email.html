First, as an at-large instructor, your instructor profile will be available to ADVANCE Center Directors who are looking for instructors. They will contact you if they have a need using the information in your profile. If you agree to teach with an ADVANCE Center, you must abide by the rules of that center in accordance with the leadership and expectations of their director. Scheduling, expected enrollment, potential compensation, and other issues will be decided between you and the Center Director. The ADVANCE Office is here to help, but only a local Center will know many of the specifics that you will need to prepare and teach your class.


To create and edit your instructor-at-large profile, including to remove your availability, login to JOSEPH using the link below.


Once contracted to teach for a center, you and your students will have access to JOSEPH, a custom database for the ADVANCE program. Through JOSEPH you can plan and manage your courses, communicate with students, access the Gateway Seminary library, assign final grades, and access additional teaching resources to build your understanding and help your students do the same.
